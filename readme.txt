Add tracking number and carrier name to WooCommerce, display tracking info at order history page, auto import tracking numbers to 17TRACK.

== Description ==

17TRACK offers an online package tracking service which has integrated tracking data with hundreds of logistics carriers. The website has about 1 million to 800K unique visitors per day and our app has a total of 8 million installs and 10K new installs everyday. Our audience are spreading all over the world, most of them are online merchants and also their customers. The service will allow these merchants to track their orders and notify their customers seamlessly.

* Auto-tracking with more than 400 carriers supported
Automatically track the status of your orders, detect the carriers and translate the tracking results with more than 40 languages supported, to help you track your shipments quickly and easily.

* Synchronize orders from your stores automatically to save your time and effort!
We provide you with a one-stop place to manage your store easily. Orders from mainstream platforms: Aliexpress, DHgate, Lazada, Wish, Ebay, Shopee can be synchronized automatically. The users can also manually set the synchronization time interval, the store nickname, etc.

* Anomalies are automatically sent to your email every day
Most sellers will care：how many packages have not been updated for a long time? How many packages may be returned? You do not have to worry anymore，we will automatically sent these to your email every day

* Tips to read before you use 17TRACK
1、Send us an email if you have any questions and we will provide support.

2、17TRACK DOES NOT fulfil your orders automatically. If you want to do this ，try to contact the actual carrier

3、Our data comes from the transport official website, so sometimes it may not be able to check, try again later
